U.S. Birth Rates

The 1973 Roe v. Wade ruling guaranteed abortion access under federal law for nearly 50 years. On June 24, 2022, the Supreme Court's Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health decision reversed that protection, returning authority to individual states. This map tracks total births across all 50 states from 1995–2024. Tile fill encodes birth volume; tile borders show each state's abortion law category.
